Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

  • What is V-Launch Pad?

    V-Launch Pad is a flagship business idea and startup pitching competition hosted by VIT-AP University, fostering innovation and collaboration among entrepreneurs and students.

  • When and where is the event taking place?

    April 4–5, 2025, at the Student Activity Center (SAC) Building, VIT-AP University, Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh.

  • Who can participate in the competition?

    Aspiring entrepreneurs, early-stage startups, and students interested in entrepreneurship.

  • How do I register for the event?

    Register through the official VIT-AP University website by February 15, 2025.

  • What is the registration fee?

    A nominal fee of ₹4750 is charged for participation.

Event Tracks

  • What are the different competition tracks?

    There are three tracks: Ideation Contest, Startup VC Pitching, and VIT-AP Idea Pitching.

  • What are the prizes for the competition?

    Winners receive ₹1 Lakh each along with mentorship, mementos, and funding opportunities.

  • Do participants get guidance before the competition?

    Yes, orientation sessions will be held from February 17 to March 7, 2025.

Submission and Screening

  • What documents or materials are required for submission?

    An initial proposal or pitch deck is required by February 20, 2025.

  • How are finalists selected?

    Finalists are chosen through preliminary screening held between March 15–20, 2025.

On the Event Days

  • What activities are planned during the event?

    Activities include an opening ceremony, presentations, networking, and awards ceremony.

  • Are there networking opportunities?

    Yes, participants can connect with investors, mentors, and industry leaders.

Logistics and Assistance

  • Is accommodation provided for outstation participants?

    Details will be shared closer to the event. Contact organizers for assistance.

  • Who can I contact for more information?

    Email: convenor.vlaunchpad@vitap.ac.in
