Why V-LaunchPad?

V-LaunchPad is more than just a business pitching competition—it’s a transformative journey for aspiring entrepreneurs, startups, and innovators. Here’s why V-LaunchPad stands out:

Key Benefits

Global Networking

Connect with investors, mentors, and industry leaders to unlock global opportunities.

Expert Mentorship

Get hands-on guidance from industry experts to shape and scale your business.

Funding Access

Compete for cash prizes, seed funding, and venture capital support.

Global Exposure

Showcase your ideas to a global audience and build credibility in the ecosystem.

Collaborative Ecosystem

Partner with like-minded innovators and access a thriving entrepreneurial network.

Recognition

Gain recognition as a leader in innovation and entrepreneurship.

Who Should Attend?

Students

Showcase your innovative ideas and gain mentorship for future success.

Startups

Pitch your business and secure funding to accelerate your growth.

Investors

Discover groundbreaking startups and connect with visionary entrepreneurs.

Mentors

Guide upcoming entrepreneurs and share your expertise with innovators.

Academicians

Encourage students to participate and foster an entrepreneurial mindset.

Corporate Professionals

Explore partnerships and stay ahead with cutting-edge innovations.
