V-Launch Expo

The expo scheduled for April 4, 2025, as part of the V-Launch Pad 2025 event, promises to be a cornerstone for fostering innovation and collaboration among entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders. Hosted at the Student Activity Center (SAC) Building, VIT-AP University, this expo offers a dynamic platform for startups and participants to showcase their groundbreaking ideas and interact with key stakeholders in the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Expo Highlights

Startup Stalls

Participants of the Startup VC Pitching track can demonstrate their products to investors and industry leaders.

Networking Opportunities

A hub for entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders to connect and explore strategic partnerships.

Mentoring Sessions

Gain insights from global partners like the Wadhwani Foundation and Fujitsu Accelerator.

The V-Launch Expo is more than an exhibition; it is a platform for meaningful connections, knowledge exchange, and entrepreneurial success. Join us to be at the forefront of innovation and become part of a thriving ecosystem dedicated to shaping the future of entrepreneurship.
