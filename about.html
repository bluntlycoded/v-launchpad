About V-LaunchPad

V-LaunchPad is the flagship business pitching competition of VIT-AP University. It aims to empower students, early-stage startups, and aspiring entrepreneurs to turn innovative ideas into impactful ventures.

Since its inception, V-LaunchPad has provided a platform for creativity, collaboration, and growth, fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in India and beyond.
